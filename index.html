    I am a PhD scholar (Junior Research Fellow) in Computer Science at BITS Pilani, Goa, working on real-time energy monitoring and sustainability-driven research. My research focuses on Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring (NILM), where I apply machine learning, signal processing, and data analytics to disaggregate appliance-level energy consumption from aggregate electrical signals.

    I design and deploy real-time monitoring systems and dashboards to support data-driven energy optimization and carbon footprint reduction across campus-scale infrastructures. My academic interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, power systems, and sustainable computing, with the goal of producing impactful research for both academia and real-world deployment.
